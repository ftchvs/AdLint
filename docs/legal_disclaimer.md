# Legal Disclaimer

AdLint is decision-support software for preflight review of ads, landing pages,
and growth campaigns. It is not legal advice, does not replace counsel or a
compliance team, and does not guarantee approval by Google, TikTok, LinkedIn,
Meta, or any other platform.

Health, privacy, and statutory findings are routed as `requires_review`. They
should be treated as prompts for qualified human review, not as determinations
that a legal violation has occurred.

Raw submissions are not persisted by default. Users can explicitly enable
logging in their own workflows for evaluation or audit purposes.
